Upwell

Upwell is a game that is intended to be fun and educational at the same time.
The goal of the game is to finish all levels like a classical platformer.
The serious aspect of the game is to educate the player along the way on the topic of mental health and depression in particular.
The game mechanics of a typical platformer together with the story are intended to engage the player to make the experience of learning more fun.
To proceed in the game, the player needs to be able to answer facts around the topic of mental health.
The required information to answer the questions is provided in the form of hints or conversations throughout the game.
To encourage the player to pay close attention to the information provided, the player is set back within the level if a question is answered incorrectly.
The successful experience of unlocking the next part of the level when a question is answered correctly, might increase the learning effect.
The Level design serves as a metaphor for the mental journey of the main character - Going from low light and dark environment to an increasingly brighter mood.
The starting sequence is a breathing exercise to help the player relax, fitting into the concept of improving well-being.
The music is selected to have a calming effect as well.
